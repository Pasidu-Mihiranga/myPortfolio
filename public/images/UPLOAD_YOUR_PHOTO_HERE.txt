INSTRUCTIONS FOR UPLOADING YOUR PHOTO:

1. Save your photo in this folder
2. Name it exactly: "profile-photo.jpg" or "profile-photo.png"
3. Recommended size: 784x1045 pixels (will be auto-resized)
4. Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

Your photo will appear on the right side of the landing page on desktop screens.

After uploading, restart the dev server with: npm run dev
